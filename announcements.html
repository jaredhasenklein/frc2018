Welcome to the 2017-2018 season: Hydrodynamics!

We're getting our website ready

Welcome! Hydrodynamics will be released in late August of 2017. As soon as the game is released, we will create resources as fast as possible and post them here. Check the home page for our planned resources. Items will say "Coming Soon" below them if they are dependant on the game and thus not created yet.

We will be using this page for both internal announcements and also to post updates from the community. If you have something you want us to say, let us know with the contact form below.

Help support this site!

Our resources are free and will always remain free. We will never clutter with ads or anything of that sort.
