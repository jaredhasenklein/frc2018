Our ad and cookie policy

Thank you for being loyal users of our content. If you don't know me, my name is Jared and I'm a senior in high school. Since 2015, I have been creating resources for robotics competitions (FLL, FTC, FRC, and Vex). This is really fun for me and I plan to continue doing this for as long as I can.

My content has always been ad free. Thanks to the support of Github, my hosting is free. Still, I have incurred many costs along the way. My domain is not cheap and I have to store a lot of content. The time commitment has also increased a lot as I work to improve quality.

At this time, I have decided to put small ads in some locations on the website. These ads will likely generate very little money but hopefully can help offset some of my costs. I would really appreciate if you whitelisted us on your Ad Blocker if you use one. The ads are tailored based on your search history with Google so we are not responsible and have not control over the content that appears. If you see anything problematic, please let me know.

Our ads are served by Google's AdSense platform. They have their own cookie policy. Google tracks your behavior to better serve you relevant ads. They control much of the online ad market so odds are, you are constantly being served these ads. You can opt out of the tracking here.

Lastly, other third party vendors may use cookies to improve your experience. This includes most things embeded from an outside source - including but not limited to Google Docs and One Drive files. We do not track you independently. Please see individual policies for details.

Thanks again! Let us know if you have questions/comments.
